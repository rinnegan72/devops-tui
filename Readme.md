# Devops TUI

There are many kubernetes based tooling and aws based tooling in the market at the moment
from open lense to k9s and aws console, but none of them can open many contexts at once
or even prooperly interact with argocd to sync applications or any other
argocd specific operations or aws specific operations.

## Why

As a devops engineer heavily invested in kubernetes, argocd and aws I have decided to make
a TUI based application that will fullfill my needs and just in general as a personal
project to learn ratatui crate

---

## What is MCK?

TUI based tool to view and interact with kubernetes based resources and argocd resources
as well as interacting with EKS, RDS and Elasticache and checking their status
